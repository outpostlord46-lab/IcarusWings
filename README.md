# Icarus Wings

A Fabric mod for Minecraft 1.21.1 that introduces two unique tiers of flight: the **Icarus Wings** and the **Mechanical Wings**. 

## 🛠 Features

### 🪽 The Wings
* **Icarus Wings**: Lightweight and agile. Be careful—don't fly too close to the sun! If they reach 1 durability, they will shatter and be lost forever.
* **Mechanical Wings**: Built for durability. These wings are heavy and provide built-in fall protection, but their weight will slow you down while walking on the ground.

### 🔧 Mechanics
* **Advanced Repairing**: Mechanical Wings don't just disappear. When they "break," they become unusable and change their model to a frayed version. They can be fully repaired in an **Anvil** using **Iron Ingots**.
* **Shock Absorption**: The heavy frame of the Mechanical Wings reduces incoming fall damage by **50%**, even when you aren't gliding.
* **Grounded Weight**: To balance their power, Mechanical Wings apply a **15% movement speed penalty** while you are walking on the ground.
* **Firework Safety**: Mechanical Wings are too heavy to be boosted by standard Firework Rockets, encouraging a different style of flight.

### 🏆 Advancements
* **Don't Fly Too Close!**: Triggered by obtaining your first set of Icarus Wings.
* **Industrial Upgrade**: A challenge advancement awarded when you craft the Mechanical Wings.

---

## 🚀 Installation

1.  Ensure you have the **Fabric Loader** installed for Minecraft 1.21.1.
2.  Download the latest release `.jar` from the [Releases] page.
3.  Place the `.jar` file into your Minecraft `%appdata%/.minecraft/mods` folder.
4.  Launch the game and take to the skies!
